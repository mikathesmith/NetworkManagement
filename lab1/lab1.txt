1. sudo(8) and sudo_root(8)
   Advantages/disadvantages of using sudo, and of leaving the root account
   disabled.
   -sudo allows a user to execute a command as the superuser.
   -advantage - allows access to everything
   -disadvantage - great power, great responsibility! Doesn't just affect you,
    could affect large number of users.
